What makes a Great Student ?

MIS students are a different breed. We're inquisitive, bold and devoted. We care about the community and the world around us.

We are doers, thinkers and creators. We belong to a close-knit MIS family that recognizes and celebrates our differences while providing the tools for success in college and beyond. Each student molds his or her own journey at MIS, and students' time here will always be a part of them.
